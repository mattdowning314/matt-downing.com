Smart Bathroom Pod

Goal: Create a bathroom that has smart lighting and controls for use in hospitals
Challenge: electronics cannot be exposed because bathroom must be easily cleaned, ease of retrofit in current hospitals

Smart Keyboard

Goal: Create a keyboard that could have virtual ‘keys’ registered by one sensor and processed on a microcontroller, activate LED under button area pressed
Challenge: Learning a new sensor technology, reliability of use, material constraints
Success: Keyboard was successfully created and detected ‘key’ presses with accuracy, leds below the pressed ‘key’ went through multiple design iterations
